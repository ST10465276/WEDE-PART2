Volunteer with TM Legal:

If I volunteer with TM Legal, what will I do?

Volunteering with us involves community workshops, legal research, client support, or adminstration which all depends on your skills and interests.

You will be required to work two days in a week.

Benefits of volunteering?

Through your voluntary work you get to make and see a real difference to disadvantaged members of our communities, gain practical legal experience especially for law studuents, and also get to grow your skills as well as strengthening your CV.

What is TM Legal looking for from their potential volunteers?

  • Dedication
  • Reliability
  • Confidence
  • Compassion

From Law students, graduates, or community members who are eager to support our mission.
